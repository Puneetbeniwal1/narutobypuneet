Naruto Anime Information

Summary

Naruto is a Japanese anime series based on the manga by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. Along with his friends and fellow ninja, Naruto goes on a series of adventures and battles to protect his village and become stronger.
